Vijay (CM from TVK)

In 2023, Vijay was seen in the action drama film Varisu directed by Vamshi Paidipally.[108][109] The film was a commercial success, grossing over ₹300 crore at the box office.[110][111] His next release of 2023 was the action thriller Leo.[112][113] It received mixed reviews from critics, but went on to become a commercially successful film and emerged as one of the highest grossing Indian films of the year with a gross more than ₹600 crore.[114][115] His action thriller film The Greatest of All Time opened to mixed reviews, but had a theatrical revenue of more than ₹450 crore.[116][117][118] In February 2024, Vijay announced that he would do only one more film, later titled Jana Nayagan.[119][120][121] The film was scheduled for release on 9 January 2026, but was delayed due to censorship issues. It was subsequently leaked on piracy websites on 9 April 2026, prior to its official theatrical release.[
